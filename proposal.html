Movie Review/Finding Web App

RESTFul API's your team will potentially use?

- Our team will be using the IMDB Rest API, to fetch and retrive movies and movie data.

How might the data you receive from the API provide insight and information to the end-user?

- The data we retrieve will be displayed to our end users, with images to show users which movies are which and to display rating/reviews made outside of our site.

What data will you collect from authenticated site users?

- We will collect the following data from authenticated site users: Ratings, Comments, Preferences, Surveys, Recommendations, ...

Identify all entities/tables you might use in your database along with their respetive attributes.

Authen Table -> password, userName, fname, lname, email
Movies Table -> movieName, ratingNum, releaseDate, director, description, genre
User Table -> fname, lname, userName
Preferences Table -> userName, genre
Review Table -> movieTitle, userName, reviewTitle, reviewComment
Rating Table -> movieTitle, userName, ratingNum
SavedMovies Table -> userName, movieTitle
Actor Table -> movieTitle, actorName

Who will potentially use your site?

- Our target audience is movie enthusiasts that enjoy binge watching new and old movies.

What are the goals of your site? Provided a minimum of 2 goals.

- Our goal is to provide the user with the option to review the movie they have watched and allow them to look through new collections depending on their preferences.

Why did your team select this topic?

- We all like watching movies and always have a hard time finding the best one. This application would allow us to look for new movies easily.
